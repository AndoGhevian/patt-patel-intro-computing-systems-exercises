### 2.45 Convert the following unsigned binary numbers to hexadecimal.
1. a. 1101 0001 1010 1111
1. b. 001 1111
1. c. 1
1. d. 1110 1101 1011 0010

#### Solution

Each hex digit corresponds to representation of 4 binary digits and vice versa.

And also hex number representation could be replased digit by digit
for 4 bit representations and vice versa.

#### a. 1101 0001 1010 1111

xd1cf

#### b. 001 1111

001 = 00001 because it is unsigned integer representation, it could SEXT by 0's.

So we have:

x1f

#### c. 1

x1 = 1, is obvious

#### d. 1110 1101 1011 0010

xedb2
