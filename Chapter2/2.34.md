### 2.34 Compute the following:
1. a. NOT(1011) OR NOT(1100)
1. b. NOT(1000 AND (1100 OR 0101))
1. c. NOT(NOT(1101))
1. d. (0110 OR 0000) AND 1111

#### a.
0100 OR 0011 = 0111

#### b.
1100 OR 0101 = 1101 =>

1000 AND 1101 = 1000 =>

NOT(1000) = 0111

#### c.
NOT(1101) = 0010 =>

NOT(0010) = 1101 note, result after two bitwise NOT is the same as input.

#### d.
(0110 OR 0000) AND 1111 = 0110 AND 1111 = 0110
