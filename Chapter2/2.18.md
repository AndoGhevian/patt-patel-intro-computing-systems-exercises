### 2.18 Add the following unsigned binary numbers. Also, express the answer in decimal.
1. a. 01 + 1011
1. b. 11 + 01010101
1. c. 0101 + 110
1. d. 01 + 10

#### Solution

because they are unsigned, sign-extension operation will always
prepend meaningless 0's even if 1 is at most significant bit.

#### a.

01 = 1

1011 = 11

0001 + 1011 = 1100 = 12

#### b.

11 = 3

01010101 = 85

00000011 + 01010101 = 01011000

#### c.

0101 = 5

110 = 6

0101 + 0110 = 1011 = 11

#### d.

01 = 1

10 = 2

01 + 10 = 11 = 3
