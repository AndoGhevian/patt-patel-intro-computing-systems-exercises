### 2.30 Compute the following. Write your results in binary.
1. a. 01010111 AND 11010111
1. b. 101 AND 110
1. c. 11100000 AND 10110100
1. d. 00011111 AND 10110100
1. e. (0011 AND 0110) AND 1101
1. f. 0011 AND (0110 AND 1101)

These are bitwise AND operations, which means logical binary
operation AND perform on bit-wise bases.

#### a.
01010111

#### b.
100

#### c.
10100000

#### d.
00010100

#### e.
(0011 AND 0110) AND 1101 = 0010 AND 1101 = 0000

#### f.
0011 AND (0110 AND 1101) = 0011 AND 0100 = 0000
